Switzerland can be a country of contrasts. Rugged mountains wrapped with smooth roads nestle beside alpine meadows full of flowers. Summer sunshine strikes the top of snow along the horizon.

Those valleys are too tempting not to hike through, those forests too interesting not to explore and the mountains are just begging to be conquered.

Whether you go by bike or choose to make the journey on two feet, you’re in a country that understands the urge to find out what’s over that hill.
